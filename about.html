Who We Are

Laerskool Kremetart is a Public Primary School school located in Giyani Kremetart. The school falls within Mopani East District Municipality in Limpopo. On this page, you will find all useful information about Laerskool Kremetart Public Primary School, such as fees, subjects, registration form, extra-mural activities, and more.
